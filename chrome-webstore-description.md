Workspaces & Encryption
Create dedicated workspaces to separate your work, personal, and sensitive data. Use Encrypted Workspaces with AES-256 encryption to keep critical information secure.

Smart Organization & Local Search
Organize conversations into folders, create categories, and tag chats. Use lightning-fast local search to find any prompt or conversation instantly, even across thousands of items.

Get a notification sound when the AI finishes generating a response, so you can multitask efficiently without constantly checking the tab.

Text Highlighting & Navigation
Select and highlight important parts of AI responses. Navigate between highlights with Previous/Next buttons, and your highlights persist across page reloads. Perfect for finding key information in long conversations.

Performance Boost
Struggling with slow performance in long conversations? Thread Trimming automatically hides older messages while preserving important context. Users report 73% faster scrolling in conversations with 200+ messages.

Prompt Management & History
Save your best prompts, access them with the / command, and track your full prompt history. Organize prompts by category, mark favorites, and create prompt chains.

Image Gallery & Tagging
View all generated images in one place. Tag, filter, and organize your visuals. Download in bulk and jump back to the source chat instantly.

Conversation Notes
Add rich notes to any conversation. Search by note content and keep important context organized alongside your chats.

Visual Customization
Choose from multiple themes, customize folder colors, and create colored tags. Our glassmorphism UI makes organization beautiful and intuitive.
